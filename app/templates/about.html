About NFL Rushing Predictor

Harnessing the power of machine learning to analyze and predict NFL rushing performance

Project Overview

The NFL Rushing Predictor is an advanced analytics platform that uses machine learning to predict rushing yards on any given play with remarkable accuracy.

Our model analyzes multiple factors including player attributes, game situation, defensive alignment, and player tracking data to provide accurate predictions that help coaches, analysts, and fans understand the game at a deeper level.

Offensive Factors

Defensive Factors
  • Defensive front alignment

  • Defensive back run support
  • Pre-snap adjustments
